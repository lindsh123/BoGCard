Lindsay Hwang

Major: Computer Science

Year: Freshman

Hometown: Fairfax, Virginia

Social Good Passion: Educational Access

Lindsay is passionate about increasing educational access. She has directed HackTJ, the nation's largest free high-school hackathon, and hosted Techstravaganza, a free STEM fair for Title 1 schools with low funding. Through these events and volunteer tutoring, she enjoys seeing students get excited and strives to make sure that everyone can access such opportunities.
